# Aladdin's Fortune 3D

## 🟢 When to play

Look for **strong W-I-L-D letter progress** concentrated on the **left reels** (especially Reels 1–3). Ideal targets include:

- **6+ total lit letters** on Reels 1–3
- Multiple reels already **wild** with lit letters (spins) remaining (best case: 2+ wild reels on the left side)

The more letters lit and the further left they are, the stronger the play.

![Aladdin's Fortune 3D: W-I-L-D letter meters above each reel showing lit progress](/guides/aladdins-fortune/aladdins-fortune-wilds.webp)

## 🛑 When to stop

Play off any active wild reels, then cash out once the strong left-side progress is gone. Do not chase new letters on cold reels.

## 🔍 How to check (quick/easy)

**W-I-L-D letter meters** are **clearly visible** above each of the 5 reels. Cycle through **all bet levels/denoms** because progress is bet-specific and persistent per bet.

## ⚠️ Risk & Warnings

**Bankroll on hand: 5–40 units** (depends on how many wild reels are already active)

This is a **medium risk** machine. You can get blanked or small pays while building new letters, but active wild reels (especially multiple on the left) deliver strong hits and occasional handpays. Opportunities are rarer now because the game is well-known to hustlers.

**Key factors that affect edge:**
- Number of lit letters on Reels 1–3 (left side is critical — it's a left-to-right paying game)
- Existing wild reels already active

## 🎭 Skins (same game different theme/art)

This is part of IGT’s **Wild Stays** family (similar persistence mechanic to other letter/wild collection games).

## 🎰 Gameplay Mechanics

**Aladdin’s Fortune 3D** is a **5-reel, 60-line** video slot by IGT (released around 2016) that pays **left-to-right**. The core feature is **Wild Stays 4 Plays** — a persistent letter-collection mechanic that creates clear advantage opportunities.

At the top of each reel sits a **W-I-L-D** meter with 4 positions. Every time a **Magic Lamp** symbol lands on a reel, it lights up one letter. Once all 4 letters are lit on a reel, that reel turns **completely wild** for the current spin **plus the next 3 spins**. Wild reels stay active across multiple spins, and you can have several wild reels running at the same time.

Because wild reels persist, the game state is highly visible and bet-persistent. Left reels (1, 2, and 3) matter most for big line wins.

The game also includes a **Player Decision Bonus** triggered by landing **3, 4, or 5 Bonus symbols**:
- You choose between different numbers of free spins with varying wild reel configurations (e.g., more spins with center wild vs. fewer spins with multiple outer wilds).

Base gameplay is straightforward with standard high-paying symbols (lamps, genies, Aladdin-themed icons) plus the wild collection that drives the AP value. The combination of persistent wild reels and left-to-right pay structure makes properly “heated” machines very profitable for short, targeted sessions.
