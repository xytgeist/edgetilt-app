# Buffalo Ascension

## 🟢 When to play

Look for **3,136+ ways** (reel heights = 132+, 231+, 222+). Free Games can be played at 131+. Play any **Super Stampede** pending in Reel 2 or 4 with center reel at max height.

## 🛑 When to stop

Play until the ways drop below **3,000** (or clearly below your personal threshold). Cash out once the strong reel heights are gone.

## 🔍 How to check 

The **current number of ways to win** is **large and prominently displayed** on hand pad. Check for Super Stampedes and Free Games by inserting a ticket with the highest bet amount. Cycle through bet levels — progress is persistent per denomination/bet.

## 🎰 Gameplay Mechanics

**Buffalo Ascension** is a **5-reel, ways-to-win** video slot by Aristocrat (released in 2022) that features **persistent reel expansion** on the middle three reels. Reels 1 and 5 stay at 4 positions high, while Reels 2, 3, and 4 can grow up to **7 positions high** as Arrow symbols land. **Free Games (8-20)** can be triggered on any spin by landing 3, 4, or 5 coins.

Every Arrow that lands on a middle reel increases that reel’s height by one position, boosting the total **ways to win** (up to 5,488 maximum). The game clearly displays the current ways count. When a reel hits 7 high and receives another Arrow, it triggers a feature (Stampede, Super Stampede, or Free Games) and resets to 4 high.

- **Stampede / Super Stampede**: Buffalo symbols charge across the screen turning many positions into high-paying Buffalos.
- **Free Games**: 8 spins with multiplier wilds. Can be retriggered by landing multiple coins. Reel heights persist into the bonus.
- **Progressive Jackpot**: Awarded via Gold Arrow on Reel 3 at max height.

The persistent growing reels create highly visible +EV opportunities. Higher ways and taller middle reels significantly improve hit frequency and payout potential, making this one of the most popular AP targets on modern casino floors.
